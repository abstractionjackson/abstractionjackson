# About Abstraction Jackson

## Who We Are

Abstraction Jackson is the business persona of Jackson Galan, a seasoned software engineer and educator based in San Francisco. With over a decade of experience in the IT industry, Jackson has worn many hats. He has engineered cutting-edge software solutions for both startups and enterprise corporations, tutored high school students as well as adult learners, and taught web development bootcamp classes to hundreds of students across various age groups. As a corporate consultant, he has built custom software solutions tailored to meet unique business needs.
